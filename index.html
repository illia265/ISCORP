Hey, I'm Illia, an undergraduate student at UC Merced studying Molecular and Cellular Biology!

I've always been fascinated by how diseases work and how we can get better at treating them. It's what pulled me into research and what's pushing me toward an MD-PhD. These days most of my time is spent in Prof. Dan Vigneron's lab at UCSF working on cancer metabolism imaging using hyperpolarized MRI and multinuclear MR spectroscopy, and in the Spencer Lab at UC Merced on intravital two-photon microscopy and optical tissue clearing. It's a lot of fun and I feel really lucky to be doing it.

Outside of that, Gen Core Biology is something really close to my heart. It's a platform where we make biology accessible through short-form content and it's grown to 150K+ followers and 120M+ global views. It started because it's genuinely how I learn best, and I just wanted that for other people too.
